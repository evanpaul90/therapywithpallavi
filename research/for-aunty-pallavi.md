## What's different

The old site read like a beautifully-made brochure with one page. The new one reads like a literary magazine — a slow, deliberate place with room to think.

**The homepage tells a single arc, top to bottom:**

1. A headline — *"Therapy for the ambitious life you're quietly exhausted by."* — that names the people who most often find you.
2. Your credentials, quietly stated: eight years of practice, over two hundred clients, one steady practice.
3. A piece about who you work with — the senior-professional-in-a-quiet-crisis archetype. Very specific. We hope it makes your right clients feel recognised.
4. Your About section — with **your PGDM finally foregrounded**. Dad and I believe the management background is your real differentiator. Almost no Indian therapist has that combination. It's exactly why the ambitious-professional client feels understood by you in ways other rooms can't match.
5. A quiet design moment — a single italic line: *"These aren't separate things. They pressurise each other."* — on why you work with the whole system.
6. Areas of work grouped thoughtfully ("The inner weather", "The relational work", "What we carry quietly", "The hinges of a life") instead of a flat list.
7. How sessions actually work — your numbers, your boundaries, stated plainly.
8. A short Journal with four articles (more below).
9. A final invitation: *"A free 15 minutes. That's the first step."*

There are also four separate pages: **About · How I Work · Journal · Contact** — plus a proper **Terms & Privacy** page ported from your Google Doc.

Everything works on phone too.

## What we couldn't include without you

- **Real testimonials.** Your old site had the heading but no testimonials underneath — it was the single biggest trust problem. When you're comfortable, three to five short client quotes (first names only, with their consent) will change the site more than any design change could.
- **Your actual social-media voice.** Instagram blocks us from reading your captions without logging in. If you can share a handful of your favourite IG captions, we can tune every paragraph to match how you actually write.
- **Pricing framing.** We kept your fee as *"set in our first conversation"* — it's the right move for your market — but if you ever want a softer hint at a range, tell us and we'll add it.
