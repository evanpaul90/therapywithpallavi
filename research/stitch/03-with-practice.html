WHO I WORK WITH

The clients who find me tend to arrive at a similar crossroads.

You're competent at work. Senior, often. Trusted with problems, paid well. Underneath that, something quieter has been unravelling.

Sunday evenings feel longer than they used to. You're sharper with your partner than you mean to be. Your parents' expectations still land in your body even though you've learned to laugh at them. There's a grief you never named.

You've tried journaling. Meditation apps. Maybe one therapist you ghosted after three sessions.

You don't need inspiration. You need someone who can hold the complexity of the life you've actually built.

ABOUT

I work at the intersection of inner life and the world you perform in.

I'm a counsellor and psychotherapist based in Bangalore, with eight years of practice and over 200 clients behind me. My training includes a Master's in Clinical Psychology, a PGDM, and certifications in CBT, IFS for trauma, NLP, and QPR crisis counselling.

The PGDM is the part most people don't expect. It matters less for what it taught me about business, and more for the fluency it gave me with the world my clients live in. Performance reviews. Political org dynamics. The family dinners where achievement becomes currency.

My practice is trauma-informed, evidence-based, and inclusive.

AREAS OF WORK

What we can work on together.

These aren't separate things. They pressurise each other.

The inner weather

Anxiety. Depression. Anger that sits on top of something older. The low self-worth that never quite goes away.

The relational work

Marriage and partnership. Premarital clarity. Family systems you've outgrown. Trust — giving it, re-finding it, rebuilding it after it broke.

What we carry quietly

Trauma with a name and trauma without one. Grief that never got formalised — a miscarriage, a parent's slow decline, a friendship that ended without words.

The hinges of a life

Workplace patterns you keep repeating across jobs. Life transitions: a new role, a new city, a new parent, an empty house, the end of something.

THE PRACTICE

How sessions work.

FORMAT

Online, secure video. 60 minutes per session.

CADENCE

Most clients start weekly. We settle into the right rhythm together once the work is moving.

FEES

Set in our first conversation, based on the work you're bringing and what's sustainable for you. Paid 24 hours in advance via UPI or bank transfer.

CANCELLATION

24+ hours: no charge. 12–24 hours: 50%. Under 12 hours or no-show: full fee applies.

These aren't punitive. They're what makes the container hold.
